# EKP Brainstorming: 20 Advanced "Bet Map" Goals

To deepen the EKP's Bet Map, we must move beyond simply "viewing data" into **Proactive Orchestration** (Horizontal alignment, Real-world monitoring, Autonomous execution).

Here are 20 strategic goals spanning Upstream Monitoring, Organizational Integration, and Downstream Sensing, instantly ranked by the **4 Big Product Risks**:
**(V) Value | (U) Usability | (F) Feasibility | (B) Business Viability**

### Risk Heatmap Analysis
*   **High Feasibility Risk (Danger Zone)**: Using the Graph for *Agentic Execution (#16)* or *Viral Sentiment Scraping (#12)* relies heavily on immature external LLMs and chaotic public data. We should probably avoid making these our core Bet Map goals to preserve engineering feasibility.
*   **High Value / Low Feasibility / High Reputational Impact (Sweet Spot)**: Goals like *Finance to Procurement Dollar Risk (#7)* and *S&OP to Marketing Alignment (#6)* use data LEGO already owns, mapping it across boundaries that usually require 3 weeks of meetings to cross.
