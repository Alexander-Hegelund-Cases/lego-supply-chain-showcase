### 📖 Supporting Evidence: The Technical Architecture Execution
*   **FAIR(EST) by Design**: Every node (Supplier, Factory, Set) includes mandatory metadata: `source`, `dataConfidence`, `lastUpdated`, and `is_synthetic`. This ensures that even in a prototype, data lineage and trust are first-class citizens.
*   **Scalability**: We optimised the ingestion pipeline to handle **26,000+ LEGO Sets** and **490+ Themes** using batch processing (`UNWIND`), proving that the EKP can handle enterprise-scale datasets.
*   **The Hidden Risk (Tier 2 Strategy)**: We extended beyond basic SAP bounds by synthetically modelling "suppliers of suppliers" to uncover deep-tier ESG bottlenecks.
